About Us

At parkXpress our mission is to transform parking into a seamless and sustainable experience. We are dedicated to make cities more livable and reducing environmental footprint associated with parking.

What do you offer?

Sensor Technology

For businesses,we offer in-depth analytics and reporting,powered by advanced sensor data,to optimize parking operations,improve efficiency,and reduce costs

Security System

Never waste another minute searching for a parking space. Our state-of-the-art guidance system directs you to available parking spots in real-time.

Reservation System

Planning a trip or a night out?Reserve your parking spot in advance with confidence,knowing that our sensor data ensures a guaranteed space for you.

Contactless payment

Say goodbye to fumbling for coins or waiting in line at parking kiosks. Our contactless payment options make parking easy and convenient.
